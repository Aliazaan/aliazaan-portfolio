Insights — Short tips (SEO & Shopify)

Actionable, short posts you can publish as blog entries to attract organic clients.

How I reduced a Shopify store load time from 7s to 2s

Compress hero images to WebP, lazy-load below-the-fold images, remove unused apps, and inline critical CSS for fast first render. These changes often produce the largest immediate gains.

3 quick SEO fixes for Shopify stores

Add structured product schema, optimize title/meta per collection, and create pillar content for your most valuable keywords. Small on-page wins compound over months.

Why size recommendation reduces returns

Giving customers a clear size suggestion — based on brand-specific fit data — reduces uncertainty and return rate dramatically. Combine with visual size guides for best results.
